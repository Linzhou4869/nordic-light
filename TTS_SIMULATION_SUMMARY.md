## Accessibility Use Case

This simulation demonstrates how distributed teams can receive build reports through audio notifications:

### Benefits
- **Accessibility:** Team members with visual impairments can receive reports
- **Multitasking:** Listen to reports while commuting or doing other work
- **Global Teams:** Audio notifications work across time zones
- **Inclusive:** Multiple format options (text + audio) for different preferences

### Integration Options
1. **Email Attachment:** Include MP3 in weekly email digest
2. **Slack/Teams Bot:** Post audio file to team channel
3. **Podcast Feed:** Generate RSS feed with weekly episodes
4. **Phone Call:** Use Twilio/Vonage to call team members
5. **Smart Speaker:** Push to Alexa/Google Home skills
