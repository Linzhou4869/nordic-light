### Immediate Actions (Next 30 Days)

1. **Belts Category Intervention**
   - Launch targeted 30-40% promotion
   - Implement bundle offers with complementary categories
   - Review and rationalize underperforming SKUs

2. **Winter Coat Clearance**
   - Maintain planned markdown cadence
   - Consider flash sales for remaining inventory

3. **Summer Collection Monitoring**
   - Track competitor launch timing weekly

   - Evaluate promotional calendar effectiveness
   - Consider dynamic pricing for fast-fashion categories

   - Review in-store belt display and visibility
