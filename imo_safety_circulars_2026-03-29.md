### 1.2 Pilot Transfer Arrangements (SOLAS Chapter V, Regulation 23)

**Status:** ADOPTED
**Entry into Force:** 1 January 2028

#### Key Safety Measures:
- New mandatory performance standards for pilot transfer equipment and arrangements
- Requirements for design, manufacture, construction, and installation
- Inspection, stowage, maintenance, replacement, and familiarization requirements
- **Mechanical pilot hoists are FORBIDDEN**
- Adequate lighting required for all pilot transfer areas and deck embarkation points
- Pilots may refuse to use non-compliant transfer arrangements

#### Associated Circulars:
- **MSC Circular** - Required pilot transfer arrangements for pilots and other personnel
- **MSC Circular** - Voluntary early implementation of the amendment to SOLAS regulation V/23

#### Consequential Amendments:
- 1994 and 2000 High-Speed Craft Codes (Record of Equipment)
- 2008 Code of Safety for Special Purpose Ships (SPS Code)
- 2005 Code of Safety for Fishermen and Fishing Vessels

### 1.4 Enhanced Programme of Inspections (ESP Code) - Remote Inspection Techniques

**Status:** Approved for adoption at MSC 111

#### Key Changes:
- Allows use of Remote Inspection Techniques (RIT) including:
  - Drones
  - Remotely operated vehicles (ROVs)
  - Crawlers
- Enables quicker, safer, and more efficient inspections
- Allows close-up surveys and ultrasonic thickness measurements in hard-to-reach areas

#### Associated Circular:
- **MSC.1/Circ.1502/Rev.1** - Revised guidance on pressure testing of cargo oil tank boundaries under the master's direction

### 1.5 Life-Saving Appliance (LSA) Code Amendments

**Status:** Approved for adoption at MSC 111
**Application:** From 1 January 2031 (new free-fall lifeboats)

#### Key Changes:
- Design and prototype test requirements for free-fall lifeboat release systems
- Simulated launching requirements (without actual water launching)
- Annual thorough examination and testing requirements

#### Associated Circulars:
